Answer: Yes

2. Question 2: Why do security analysts refine alert rules? Select two answers.

Answer: To improve the accuracy of detection technologies and to reduce false positive alerts

Answer: Analysis

4. Question 4: What are some causes of high alert volumes? Select two answers.

Answer: Broad detection rules and misconfigured alert settings
